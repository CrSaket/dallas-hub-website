What We've Built

Growing the Game Across North Texas

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, all our achievements are powered by our players, families, coaches, and donors.

1000+

Players Registered

More than one thousand youth players are currently registered with Dallas Hub programs across the North Texas region, making us the single largest youth cricket organization in the area.

80+

Competitive Teams

Over eighty teams compete under the Dallas Hub banner in regional and national USA Cricket-sanctioned competitions, representing players from under-10 through under-19 age groups.

20

Cricket Academies

Twenty dedicated cricket academies operate across North Texas under the Dallas Hub umbrella, each providing structured coaching, training, and pathway programs for youth players.

Community Impact

Building Community Through Sport

Dallas Hub serves one of the most diverse metropolitan regions in the United States. Cricket is uniquely positioned to bring together communities from South Asia, the Caribbean, the UK, and beyond — and that's exactly what we do across North Texas every day.

As a nonprofit, we actively work to reduce barriers to entry, offering scholarship programs, equipment drives, and subsidized registrations.

USA Cricket Pathways

Pathways to National Representation

As an official USA Cricket hub, Dallas Hub provides structured pathways for elite players to progress through regional selection pools and into national youth team consideration.

Our coaches operate under USA Cricket certified frameworks, ensuring every player in North Texas receives training that meets national standards.
