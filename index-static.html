Who We Are

Dallas Hub Cricket Association is a registered nonprofit organization and an officially recognized youth hub under USA Cricket. We are the biggest youth cricket association in all of North Texas, serving the greater Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area.

Founded with the mission of developing the next generation of cricket talent, Dallas Hub operates across 20 academies, supports more than 80 competitive teams, and engages over 1,000 players throughout the North Texas region.

As a nonprofit, every dollar we raise goes directly back into our programs, infrastructure, and the players we serve. We rely on the generosity of our community, corporate sponsors, and dedicated volunteers.

Dallas Hub is committed to providing equal access to cricket for all youth in North Texas, regardless of background or prior experience. Cricket teaches discipline, teamwork, and resilience — values that extend far beyond the field.

What We Offer

Our Programs

Structured cricket development for all ages and skill levels — from first-time players to competitive regional athletes across North Texas.

All programs administered in partnership with USA Cricket

Junior Foundations

Ages 6–10

Our entry-level program introduces young players to the fundamentals of cricket in a fun, safe, and encouraging environment. No prior experience required.

  • Basic batting, bowling, and fielding skills
  • Age-appropriate modified cricket formats
  • Focus on fun and sportsmanship
  • Qualified junior-level coaches
  • Saturday morning sessions available

Development Academy

Ages 10–14

Bridges recreational play and competitive cricket, building technical skills and game awareness through structured coaching.

  • Technical skill development
  • Introduction to tactics and game formats
  • Inter-academy competitive fixtures
  • USA Cricket certified coaches
  • Strength and conditioning components

Elite Performance Program

Ages 14–19

Designed for high-potential players aspiring to represent North Texas at regional and national levels. Entry by selection.

  • Advanced technical and tactical training
  • Regional tournament competitions
  • USA Cricket pathway preparation
  • Mental performance coaching
  • Video analysis sessions

Girls Cricket Program

All Ages

Dallas Hub is committed to growing women's cricket in North Texas. Our dedicated girls' programs provide a welcoming and empowering environment.

  • Female-specific coaching environment
  • All age groups from 8 to 19
  • Regional women's tournaments
  • Mentorship from senior players
  • Pathway to USA Cricket women's programs

Schools Outreach

Ages 8–18

We bring cricket to North Texas schools through in-curriculum and after-school partnerships, introducing the sport to new communities.

  • In-school cricket clinics
  • After-school training programs
  • Teacher and PE coach education
  • Free equipment and materials provided
  • Pathway to Dallas Hub academies

Coach Education

Coaches

Dallas Hub runs USA Cricket-aligned coach education and certification programs for aspiring and current coaches across North Texas.

  • USA Cricket Level 1 and Level 2 certification
  • Child safeguarding training
  • Ongoing coaching development workshops
  • Mentorship by senior coaches
  • Access to national coaching resources
