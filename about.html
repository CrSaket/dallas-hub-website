Who We Are

Dallas Hub Cricket Association is a registered nonprofit organization and an officially recognized youth hub under USA Cricket. We are the biggest youth cricket association in all of North Texas, serving the greater Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area.

Founded with the mission of developing the next generation of cricket talent, Dallas Hub operates across 20 academies, supports more than 80 competitive teams, and engages over 1,000 players throughout the North Texas region.

As a nonprofit, every dollar we raise goes directly back into our programs, infrastructure, and the players we serve. We rely on the generosity of our community, corporate sponsors, and dedicated volunteers.

Dallas Hub is committed to providing equal access to cricket for all youth in North Texas, regardless of background or prior experience. Cricket teaches discipline, teamwork, and resilience — values that extend far beyond the field.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

Every young person in North Texas deserves the opportunity to experience cricket. Our nonprofit status keeps participation affordable and inclusive.

Excellence

We hold our coaches, programs, and facilities to the highest standards, providing world-class development pathways for aspiring cricketers.

Community

Cricket builds bridges. We celebrate the diversity of North Texas and use cricket as a tool for community connection.

Integrity

As a nonprofit, we operate with full transparency. We are stewards of community trust and dedicated to responsible governance.

Partnerships

Our affiliation with USA Cricket ensures our players and programs meet national standards with pathways to national team consideration.

Official Affiliation

Proudly Affiliated with USA Cricket

Dallas Hub is one of the officially designated youth cricket hubs administered by USA Cricket, the national governing body for cricket in the United States. This affiliation creates pathways for our players to reach the highest levels of the game.
